ABOUT US

Sunnies and fashion go hand-in-hand. Bold and daring, melt is a Los Angeles brand of women’s sunglasses. With intricate details and signature designs, melt sunglasses offers a top selection of aviators, high fashion metals and acetate frames with revo lenses ranging in color from mirror to smoke to baby pink.

Born in 2016, melt draws inspiration from feminine-street style and is known for offering the best statement-making sunglasses that are sure to turn an eye. Whether you’re lounging by the pool, dininel-fresco or jamming outside to the latest bands, melt sunglasses are a must.

Keep your eyes open and your sunglasses on ‘cause melt is sure to be a cult favorite with influencers, celebrities and fashion girls everywhere.
